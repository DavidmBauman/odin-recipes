Hotdog!

This recipe is for a classic american hot dog, topped with the finest yellow mustard

This easy recipe will teach you how to microwave a hotdog and gently top said hotdog with yellow mustard.

This recipe calls for:

  • microwave
  • 1 plate
  • 1 hotdog bun
  • 1 precooked hotdog
  • yellow mustard
  1. Take 1 hotdog out of the package and place on plate.
  2. Put plate with hotdog on it into the microwave and cook for 1min 30.
  3. Take plate with hotdog out of the microwave and place on countertop
  4. Take 1 hotdog bun out of the packaging and place next to the hotdog.
  5. Place hotdog into the bun.
  6. Top hotdog with mustard.
